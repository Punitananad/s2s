## 🎯 Common Questions & Answers

**Q: Do guests need to download an app?**
A: No! They just scan the QR code with their phone camera. Works on any smartphone.

**Q: What if we don't have WiFi in rooms?**
A: Guests can use their mobile data. The app is very lightweight.

**Q: How much does it cost?**
A: Starting at ₹2,999/month for up to 20 rooms. Professional plan at ₹5,999/month includes all features.

**Q: Is there a setup fee?**
A: No setup fee. We help you get started for free.

**Q: How long does setup take?**
A: 1-2 hours. We provide training and support.

**Q: Can we customize the menu?**
A: Yes! You have full control over menu items, prices, and categories.

**Q: What about payment?**
A: Guests can pay at checkout or we can integrate payment gateways.

**Q: Do you provide QR codes?**
A: Yes! We generate and print QR codes for all your rooms.

**Q: What if internet goes down?**
A: Orders are queued and sync when connection returns. Staff can also take manual orders.

**Q: Can we try it first?**
A: Yes! 14-day free trial, no credit card required.
